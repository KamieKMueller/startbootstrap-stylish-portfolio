Kitkat Recipes

Kitkat recipes is a social media platform for your recipes. We offer a community pinterest like feed to display prominent recipes, with a search bar to filter out unique choices. Upon making a free account, you can post your own recipes and share them with your friends.

Futue Goals: We eventually want to add a Github style "forking" option where you can fork other people's recipes and add variations. We are also working on the backend functions of actually allowing users to make accounts.

This website was built at NHacks 2, a high school hackathon. It won best rookie hack.

  • Link: www.kitkatrecipes.tech
  • Date: June 2017
  • Platform: Web Development

Amazeballs

Amazeballs is an arcade-style 3D PC game. The objective is to navigate the ball throughout the maze, without hitting the walls, and collecting all the tokens without running out of health

Future Goals: Though the game is quite finished, it would be cool to add more levels and depth to the game (story line, more types of tokens, time)

Amazeballs was built on Unity and built for submission to CollegeBoard for AP Computer Science Principles.

  • Video Link: Amazeballs Demo Vid
  • Date: May 2017
  • Platform: Unity

Northville DECA Website

The Northville DECA website was made for the Northville High School DECA organization, a business competiion-based club. The website features a resource page where members can access confidential files, an announcements page, an about page, and a sponsors page.

Future Goals: the website will routinely be monitored and updated to reflect the club season. We aim to make it more user-friendly and add more functionality towards it (e.g. a contact/newsletter sign up button). We also plan on making it more secure.

  • Link: northvilledeca.com
  • Date: February 2016
  • Platform: Web Development

Molecalc

Molecalc is a chemistry help Android app, built during MHacks 8. The simple interface allows users to enter in chemistry compounds to determine molar mass as well as enter in compounds/elements of a reaction in order to predict its occurrence.

Future Goals: The app requires more cleaning up. There can be more depth and expansion added. Additionally, it can be a little more user-friendly when entering in compounds and elements.

  • Link: Devpost
  • Date: October 2016
  • Platform: Android Studio
